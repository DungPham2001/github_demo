Skipping Rope Finance - is a web mobile fitness application that integrates
Move-To-Earn mechanism through simple activity for most people.

When you join in the Skipping Rope "universe", you will not only have chances to work out daily but also participate in the world of challenges in individual or group. The Skipping Rope Metaverse's blockchain system will record your training results, convert the distance you cycled into corresponding rewards (tokens or valuable NFTs).

With these Tokens and NFTs, you can buy, sell, and exchange on the marketplace, there by converting into real income for participants. The more you practice, the more tokens and NFTs you get, which also motivates you to gain both health benefits and valuable rewards.
